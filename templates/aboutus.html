WELCOME

LU BIO VISION


Looking for a quicker, more accurate method to identify Melanoma skin cancer? Look no farther than LU Bio Vision, the framework used to evaluate and produce explainability heatmaps that has been approved by dermatologists and oncologists. You can examine your skin with LU Bio Vision and receive the answers you need seconds.

We are a research group from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Moratuwa. Our passion lies in the field of medical diagnostics, particularly in the accurate, fast, and effective identification of melanoma skin cancer with explainability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the way melanoma skin cancer is diagnosed. We strive to empower medical students to enhance their knowledge and understanding of melanoma diagnostics through our innovative products. Additionally, we aim to support doctors by providing them with a reliable second opinion through our model's predictions. We also endeavor to enable individuals to conveniently diagnose their skin conditions at home, thereby promoting early detection and intervention.

Our Team

At LU BIO VISION, we have assembled a dynamic team consisting of a Professor (Prof. Dulani Meedeniya) , a PhD student (Ms. Senuri De Silva), and two Engineering students(Uditha Isuranga , Lahiru Kumara), all from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Moratuwa. Our team members are experts in their respective fields, with a deep understanding of computer science and melanoma skin cancer diagnostics.We are passionate about utilizing cutting-edge technologies and leveraging our expertise to drive advancements in the field of melanoma diagnosis.
